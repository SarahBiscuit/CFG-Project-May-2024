Kitty Parlour




Welcome to the kitty parlour, where feline luxury meets expert care, ensuring your beloved companions enjoy a purr-fectly indulgent experience tailored just for them.



Our expert groomers cater to every cat's needs, ensuring a stress-free experience with gentle care and top-notch products. Treat your furry friend to a day of luxury and leave them feeling refreshed and fabulous!

Services Available

Wet Groom

A wet cat groom typically involves the following:
  • Bath with cat safe shampoo followed by a full brush through once dried
  • Ears and eyes cleaned
  • Claw clipping

Prices start from £40.

Dry Groom & Brushout

Our dry groom service includes everything, minus the water! You can expect the following services:
  • Optional waterless shampoo
  • Ears and eyes cleaned
  • Claw clipping

Prices start from £30.

Kitten Groom

A gentle introduction for kittens up to 6 months old. This includes:
  • Nail trim
  • Bath and blowdry
  • Comb through

Prices start from £30.

Flea Bath

We want to keep your cat healthy, happy and free them of fleas! This includes:
  • Gentle cleansing formulas
  • Flea-killing agents
  • Moisturising and conditioning products

Prices start from £50.

Claw Clipping

This is a vital aspect of your cat's grooming routine. By keeping your cat's claws trimmed, you're preventing the risk of ingrown nails.

Prices start from £10 and for £5 extra, we can trim the hair from around the pands and foot.

Matt Removal

More common in outdoor cats, matting can become uncomfortable for cats. Our service will relieve your cat from stress, leaving them healthy and happy!

Prices start from £70.
